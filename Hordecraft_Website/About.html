About Me & The server

Welcome!

Hi, I'm Wimfish1, the creator and developer of Hordecraft. I have a passion for Development and have been part of the gaming community for years. The goal with Hordecraft was to create a fairly unique and enjoyable experience for players.

How It Started

I've always been interested with development, starting at the age of 6 with Roblox Studio and progessing to HTML, CSS, JS along with Java, C# and other languages. Now that im older and have a source of income I decided i'd finally do something I've wanted to do since I was much younger, start a Minecraft server. I also wanted it to be unique so I decided to learn plugin development and thats how I got the idea of Hordecraft. Combining Rust like building systems, raiding with Minecraft graphics and Zombie apocalyse styled gameplay.
